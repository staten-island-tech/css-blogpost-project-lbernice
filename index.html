Orchids that Resemble Animals

#1: Monkey Orchids

Monkey Orchids translates to "little dragon monkey." Monkey Orchids could represent evil and death, as well as power and authority. What makes this particular flower captivating is its resemblance to a monkey's face, which is located at the base of the petals.

#2: White Egret Orchids

White Egret Orchids are known for their beautiful appearance and resemblance to a white egret (a bird) in flight. White Egret flowers typically represent innocence, purity, and grace.

#3: Flying Duck Orchids

Flying Duck Orchids are unique due to their duck-like blooms. Flying Duck Orchids may only survive with the presence of fungus. Likewise to the White Egret Orchid, it is also classified as an endangered species.
